Video Series

I created Nutshell Biblical Hebrew, a series of quick and painless videos that introduce the ancient Hebrew language. I’m a student and teacher of the Bible, and I love the challenge of language learning. Maybe you’ve always been interested in biblical languages. Maybe you took Hebrew a long time ago and want to work off the rust. Either way, I hope that these videos are helpful for you!
