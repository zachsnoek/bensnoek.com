Benjamin P. Snoek

Biography

Ben Snoek is a research-practitioner interested in the intersections of the academy and church, particularly in the area of worship studies. He graduated from Indiana Wesleyan University in Marion, Indiana, with a bachelor’s degree in Pastoral & Worship Ministries and a master’s degree in Biblical & Theological Studies. He is currently a doctoral student at Columbia International University, specializing in systematic and liturgical theology.

Ben has served churches of all sizes and styles in the Christian Reformed Church, Reformed Church in America, and Wesleyan Church, most recently as a transitional minister at Kibbie Christian Reformed Church in South Haven, Michigan. An occasional string bassist, Ben is also a published composer and a freelance graphic designer.
