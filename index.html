Benjamin P. Snoek

Biography

Ben Snoek was born in South Korea and raised in Holland, Michigan, before graduating from Indiana Wesleyan University in Marion, Indiana, with a bachelor’s degree in Pastoral & Worship Ministries and a master’s degree in Biblical & Theological Studies. Ben has served churches of all sizes and styles in the Christian Reformed Church, Reformed Church in America, and Wesleyan Church, most recently as a transitional minister at Kibbie Christian Reformed Church in South Haven, Michigan. Additionally, Ben has experience in worship leading, church media and communications, church planting, church businesses, educational ministries, and outreach and hospitality ministries. An occasional string bassist, Ben is a published composer and a freelance graphic designer.
